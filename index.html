PROJECT MANAGERS

Can you be in charge of all IT staff that aims to design, develop and implement information security and technology? If you are capable to oversee networks, data and backup systems ensuring that the information flows transparently and smoothly, you are perfect for this role!

Project Manager

CareLytix is hiring Project manager for the long term government project MDTHINK. This is an on-site government contracting position, CareLytix offer competitive pay rate. The location of this job is in Linthicum Heights, Maryland (very close to BWI airport). Candidate must come to the government office building in Linthicum Heights, Maryland for an in-person interview.

Qualifications & Experience required:

• At least five (5) years of experience in project management in IT related projects and must demonstrate a leadership role in at least three successful projects that were delivered on time and on budget.
• Experience with agile scrum, SAFe methodology, VersionOne or Jira for tracking agile epics, features, user stories, capacity, product life cycle and release management.
• Technical background and experience in Java and cloud based web application development projects is preferred.
• Demonstrates excellent writing and oral communications skills.
• Experience in Medicaid/Medicare or similar health care related organization business processes.
• Experience in maintaining requirements documentation and developing and analyzing user stories.
• Experience in developing and implementing end- to-end (application to claim) test plans.
• Experience in report development and generation.
• Preferred experience with Medicaid Management Information System (MMIS)’s eligibility and claims processing.

Education Background:

• Master’s degree or project management certification is preferred.
• A Bachelor's Degree from an accredited college or university with a major in Computer Science, Information Systems, Engineering, Business, or other related technical discipline is required.

Job Duties:

• The Project Manager is assigned the management of specific agile project teams and work performed by those teams. .
• Performs day-to-day management of the agile project teams, identifies issues and risks and recommends possible issue and risk mitigation strategies associated with the project.
• Acts as a facilitator between a State agency and IT contractor teams.
• Is responsible for ensuring that work performed is within scope, consistent with requirements, and delivered on time and on budget.
• Identifies critical paths, tasks, dates, testing, and acceptance criteria.
• Provides solutions to improve efficiency (e.g., reduce costs while maintaining or improving performance levels).
• Monitors issues and provides resolutions for up-to date status reports.
• Demonstrates excellent writing and oral communications skills.

Greenplum Architect

The successful candidate would be expected to be multi-disciplined (have breadth), and depth in information strategies, solution alternatives and technical strategies. The primary role of the Greenplum Architect is to create solution architecture, and to lead technical design and implementation of a healthcare data and analytics platform with Greenplum and related big data technical stack by taking into consideration of the business needs, objectives, required, and constraints, and or utilizing customer’s technology value systems as the foundation for solution development. The technical domain of the Greenplum Architect includes all technical aspects of the solutions including data integration, data architecture, data analytics, information delivery, as well as infrastructure, testing, technical standards, best practices, tools and technologies, security, and technical oversight of architecture, design, development, capacity planning, performance tuning, and technical administration.

You must have a thorough understanding of data warehousing and have used a variety of databases and other data sources and targets, including at least one Massively Parallel environment such as Greenplum, Netezza. Experience in Hadoop and NoSQL databases is an advantage. Must be proficient in one scripting languages, such as Shell scripting, Perl, Python. Must demonstrate the ability to understand and working with data at very large scale. Must demonstrate experience in at least one development methodology applicable to Business Intelligence space.

Qualifications & Experience required:

• Deep experience with data warehousing solutions and Big Data architectures and concepts
• Systems architecture background, with emphasis on healthcare BI, data and data analytics solutions
• Senior professional level role with hands-on enterprise level architect with deep experience in Information management, data warehousing and analytics technologies
• Greenplum architect working experience for at least 4 years
• Experience with designing systems with high scalability, high extensibility and high maintainability
• In-depth knowledge about the Greenplum hardware and architecture
• Expertise on Greenplum sizing and end to end performance tuning
• Solutions Design and installation / configuration experience with Greenplum administration
• Support multiple services and multiple databases of medium to high complexity
• Define and discern key aspects of a problem and develop an integrated solution within a broad technical and business context of significant impact
• Support the technology team to establish standards and guidelines for the design and development, deployment and maintenance of information and data analytics
• Experience with architecture tools such as Visio, Creately, Google Draw, etc
• Greenplum DCA (appliance) experience is not required
• Solution architecture design experience in healthcare industry is big plus

Education Background:

At least BS (Master/Ph. D preferred) in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Mathematics, Statistics ,Data Science, or a related field

Oracle Data Architect

The primary role of the Greenplum Architect is to lead the architecture design and implementation for the end-to-end solution by taking into consideration the business needs, objectives, required, and constraints, and or utilizing ours and customer’s technology value systems as the foundation for solution development.

The technical domain of the Greenplum Architect includes all technical aspects of the solutions including data integration, data architecture, information delivery and analytics, infrastructure, testing, technical standards, best practices, tools and technologies, security, and technical oversight of architecture, design, development, capacity planning, performance tuning, and technical administration. The successful candidate would be expected to be multi-disciplined (have breadth), and depth in information strategies, solution alternatives and technical strategies.

The ideal candidate must have a thorough understanding of Data Warehousing and Big Data architectures and concepts. Must have used a variety of Databases and other data sources and targets, including at least one Massively Parallel environment such as Greenplum, Netezza. Experience in Hadoop and NoSQL databases is an advantage. Must be proficient in one scripting languages, such as Shell scripting, Perl, Python. Must be proficient in data modeling techniques.

Qualifications:

• 5+ years of hands-on experience with architecting large scale Salesforce solutions.
• 3+ years of experience working on Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, Force.com, Chatter and App-exchange applications.
• 5+ years of experience using Apex Classes / Controllers, Apex Triggers, SOQL, SOSL, Visual Force Page/Components, Test Classes, schedule jobs, batch jobs and email services.
• 4+ years of experience with Workflow and Approvals, Dashboards, Analytic Snapshots, Custom Objects and Custom Settings, Reports, Validation rules.
• 4+ years of experience in integrating external applications with Salesforce using REST/SOAP.
• Hands on experience in Salesforce tools, such as Force.com, IDE, Eclipse IDE Plug-in, Force.com Explorer, Data Loader and Sublime.
• 4+ years of experience in packaging and deploying applications, deploying meta-data between environments using ANT and change sets.
• 2+ years of experience with Salesforce CRM solution.
• In-depth knowledge of Salesforce.com Partner and Customer communities.
• Knowledge of AJAX, JavaScript, jQuery, Highcharts, Web service API.
• Knowledge of different SDLC such waterfall, Agile, etc.
• Salesforce Developer Certification.

Experience Required:

• Deep experience with data warehousing solutions
• Systems architecture background, with emphasis on healthcare BI and analytics solutions
• Senior professional level role with hands-on enterprise level architect with deep experience in Information management, data warehousing and analytics technologies
• Greenplum architect working experience for at least 4 years
• Experience with designing systems with high scalability, high extensibility and high maintainability
• In-depth knowledge about the Greenplum hardware and architecture
• Expertise on Greenplum sizing and end to end performance tuning
• Solutions Design and installation / configuration experience with Greenplum administration
• Support multiple services and multiple databases of medium to high complexity
• Define and discern key aspects of a problem and develop an integrated solution within a broad technical and business context of significant impact
• Contributes to system design through optimal use of database components
• Support the technology team to establish standards and guidelines for the design and development, deployment and maintenance of information and data analytics
• Experience with architecture tools such as Visio, Creately, Gliffy, Google Draw, etc
• Greenplum DCA (appliance) experience is not required

Job Duties:

• Architect, design, develop and implement enterprise solutions in Salesforce. Architect, develop and enhance Salesforce CRM solutions.
• Implement enterprise system and application architecture to ensure performance, scalability, maintainability, extensibility, and application integration with external systems.
• Develop plans for migrating architectures; develop technical reference models to include hardware/software standards.
• Build Solutions, interfaces and enhancements for Credentialing Database and Learning Management Systems in Salesforce.com
• Develop integration between Salesforce applications, including Salesforce CRM Solutions and Legacy systems using Salesforce APIs.
• Create Packages to deploy code from Sandbox to Production environment; troubleshoot all issues related to code in Sandbox and Production environment.
• Develop Apex Triggers, Visualforce pages, Apex Classes.

Education Background:

Bachelor Degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Software Engineering or Information
